The tag is for ruby annotations.

Ruby characters are small, annotative characters placed above other text characters usually from
the Japanese "Kanji", Korean "Hanja", and Chinese "Hanzi".

Use together with and : The element consists of one or more characters that
needs an explanation/pronunciation, and an element that gives that information, and an
optional element that defines what to show for browsers that do not support ruby
annotations.

Example:

Ruby 漢 ㄏㄢˋ
