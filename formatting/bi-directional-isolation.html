The tag stands for bi-directional isolation.

This tag isolates a part of the text from being formatted in a different direction.

Note: The and tags are often mistakenly interchanged. Remember that is for
isolating a text from being formatted to a certain direction while formats the orientation
of a text whether from a left to right orientation (ltr) or to a right to left orientation (rlt).

See for more explanation and examples.

Example:

bdi

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
