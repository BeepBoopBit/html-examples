The tag is used to describe a reference to a cited creative work.

Note: The text in the element usually renders in "italic".

Example:

    <!DOCTYPE html>
    <html>
        <head>
            <meta charset="UTF-8">
            <meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge">
            <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
            <title>Citing Creative Works</title>
        </head>
        <body>
            <blockquote>
                <p>It was a bright cold day in April, and the clocks were striking thirteen.</p>
            </blockquote>
            <p>First sentence in <cite>Nineteen Eighty-Four</cite><br>
                by George Orwell (Part 1, Chapter 1).</p>
        </body>
    </html>
