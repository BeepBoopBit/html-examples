The

tag defines the contact information (organization name, email address, URL, physical address,
phone number, social media handle) of the owner.

This tag helps in screen reading when people try to search for the contact details of a webpage.

The text in the

element usually renders in 'italic', and browsers will always add a line break
before and after the
element.

Example:

        <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html>
            <head>
                <meta charset="UTF-8">
                <meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge">
                <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
                <title>Contact Us</title>
            </head>
            <body>
                <h1>Contact Us</h1>
                <address>
                    Ground Floor, Makati Campus Building 333 Sen. Gil Puyat St. Avenue, Makati City 1200 Philippines<br>
                    Telephone: (+632) 8247-5000 loc. 5802 <br>
                    Email: registrar@mapua.edu.ph <br>
                </address>
            </body>
        </html>
