The <bdi> tag stands for bi-directional override.

This tag is used to change the current direction of the text orientation.

This tag formats the text using:

    <p>"<bdo dir="ltr">" - for "left to right" text orientation.</p>

    <p>Example:</p>
    <p><bdo dir="ltr">These are the nights that never die.</bdo></p>

    <p>Output:</p>
    <bdo dir="ltr">These are the nights that never die.</bdo>

    <p>"<bdo dir="rtl">" - for "right to left" text orientation. </p>

    <p>Example:</p>
    <p><bdo dir="rlt">These are the nights that never die.</bdo></p>

    <p>Output:</p>
    <bdo dir="rlt">These are the nights that never die.</bdo>
